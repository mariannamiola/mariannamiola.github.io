# Resources

A collection of software tools, datasets, and references related to my research in computational geometry, geostatistics, and environmental modeling.

---

## Software & Libraries

<ul class="entry-list">

  <li>
    <div class="entry-meta">C++ Software · 2025 · Public</div>
    <div class="entry-title"><a href="https://github.com/mariannamiola/MUSEgeom">MUSE-geometry</a></div>
    Geospatially-aware tool for 2D/3D modeling of complex geometries with mesh-based techniques. Standalone version of the geometry processing core of MUSE. <em>Role: Author, designer, developer.</em>
  </li>

  <li>
    <div class="entry-meta">C++ Software & Library · 2025 · Available by request</div>
    <div class="entry-title">MUSE — Modeling Uncertainty as a Support for Environment</div>
    Stochastic-based software for modeling spatial uncertainty on unstructured meshes, integrating geostatistics and geometry processing. Designed for environmental geochemistry, geology, and engineering. TRL = 9. <em>Role: Author, designer, developer.</em>
  </li>

  <li>
    <div class="entry-meta">C++ Library · 2026 · Public</div>
    <div class="entry-title"><a href="https://github.com/DanielaCabiddu/EWOPE">EWoPe — Embeddable Workflow Persistence</a></div>
    Lightweight C++ library providing metadata support for computational workflows to ensure reproducibility and revision of scientific pipelines. <em>Role: Author, designer, developer.</em>
  </li>

  <li>
    <div class="entry-meta">C++ Software · 2022 · Available by request</div>
    <div class="entry-title">GEO3D</div>
    Software for 3D geometric modeling of stratified deposits with applications in geophysics. Developed during the GEO3D Research Fellowship at CNR-IMATI. <em>Role: Author, designer, developer.</em>
  </li>
</ul>
